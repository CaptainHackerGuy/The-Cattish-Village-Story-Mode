Plot

Your profile comes from your Cattish Village account and you can use your Cattish Village Cosmetics and new items/abilites here. You need to clear a dungeon with 50+ rooms by fighting mobs such as, Zombie, Ghast, Dogs and Creepers. The winning party/indivisual gets 10B Cattish VIllage Coins(If it is a party reward will be split). The dungeons get progressivly harder and there is a boss level every 10 rooms. The boss drops loot according to its loot table and Difficulty. The final boss is a secret :O. If you unlock checkpoints you can respawn at that specific room if you die(Checkpoints Cost 10k Ruby Each) and there are checkpoints every 10 floors. At the final boss the boss will have 3 health bars and when the boss is at its final health bar it gets a 10% weapon speed buff and 30% weapon DMG buff but it also gets a -20% speed buff.
